Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add an appliance?

Go to the Appliances tab, tap the "+" button, choose from our template library or create a custom appliance. Maintenance tasks will be auto-generated based on manufacturer recommendations.

How does iCloud sync work?

If you have iCloud enabled on your device, HomeKeep+ will automatically sync your data across all your Apple devices. No setup required.

What's included in HomeKeep+ Pro?

Pro unlocks unlimited appliances (free: 5), cost analysis charts, multi-home management, custom seasonal tasks, and PDF report export.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Go to Settings > Apple ID > Subscriptions on your iPhone, find HomeKeep+, and tap Cancel Subscription. You'll retain Pro access until the end of your billing period.

How do I restore my purchase on a new device?

Open HomeKeep+, go to the Pro upgrade screen, and tap "Restore Purchase". Make sure you're signed in with the same Apple ID used for the original purchase.

Can I export my data?

Yes. Go to Settings > Export Data to export a JSON backup, or generate a PDF maintenance report for your records.

How do I delete all my data?

You can delete all data by uninstalling the app, or use Settings > Export Data to review your data before deletion.
